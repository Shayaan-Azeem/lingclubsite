About Us

The White Oaks Linguistics Club focuses on preparing for exciting linguistics competitions like NACLO. We meet biweekly to solve fun linguistic puzzles and explore various languages. The time commitment is minimal, and everyone is welcome!

Why Join?

  • Prepare for NACLO and other competitions
  • Learn about language structures
  • Minimal time commitment
  • Have fun solving puzzles!

Competitions

Our main focus is NACLO, the North American Computational Linguistics Olympiad. It’s a fun, exciting way to explore how languages work while competing in a friendly environment. No previous experience is needed!

How to Prepare

  • Attend our biweekly meetings for tips and practice problems
  • Work through past NACLO problems
  • Join group discussions and share problem-solving strategies
